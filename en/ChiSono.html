About Me

My name is Riccardo Monsellato, and I have always had a deep passion for sport and well-being. Since the age of six, I have competed in sports, and the injuries I experienced led me toward athletic preparation and rehabilitation.

At 19, I chose to study Sports Science at university, with the goal of turning my passion into a profession. Later, I attended a course organized by A.C. Chievo Verona and sponsored by Lega Calcio Serie A, focused on athlete load management. Soon after, I earned my diploma in MCB massage and hydrotherapy from St. George School in Darfo Boario Terme (BS) in 2016.

Since 2017, I have continued my training through courses organized by Atalanta B.C., where I had the opportunity to learn from top-level professionals in the field.

Today I work in my studio as an MCB massage therapist, kinesiologist and personal trainer, guiding people through personalized paths toward their health and performance goals.

If you would like to start a program tailored to your needs, get in touch: I will be happy to arrange a meeting and meet you in person.
I look forward to meeting you.
